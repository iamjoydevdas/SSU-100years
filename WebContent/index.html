Our Club

The Havelocks Sports Club was born when 27 school boys of ages ranging from 12 to 16 years whose homes were in the vicinity of Havelock Park agreed to organize themselves into a Club and thus the Havelocks Sports Club came into existence. The new club had no constitution, no office bearers, no materials, no grounds, nothing but enthusiasm and comradeship. The inspiration and keenness for all this came from none other than a boy named Roy Joachim and so in 1915 Roy together with the other boys got about setting up the club never dreaming that their juvenile efforts would reach maturity and that the club would live to take its place in the front ranks of the local sporting world- celebrating a 100 years.More
